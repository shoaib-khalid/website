Refund Policy

 

We understand that sometimes things don’t always go according to plan, so we have made this Refund Policy to help you!

WRONG AND DAMAGE ITEM: We do our absolute best to ensure that the product arrives at our customers not damaged. If you receive an item that is damaged or in wrong color/ size than the one you originally order, please email us a photo to show proof of this within 48 hours at help@symplified.biz (with subject REFUND) after receiving your order and we will file a claim on your behalf. Once your photo is received and inspected, we will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed.

CANCELLATION & RETURNS: We do not accept cancellation & returns on any purchase after the orders are placed and paid.

Refunds (if applicable): Once your photo along with supporting reason of refund is received and investigated, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund request. If your refund request is approved, we will then submit to our Finance Dept for refund processing, and the credit will be refunded to your credit card or original method of payment within 10 working days.
Note: The default refund period is up to 180 days from the transaction date. Any refund request after this period will no longer be entertained.

Invalid Reasons:

We are not responsible to any misroute or undeliverable parcels because of missing, incomplete, or incorrect destination information. Please enter the correct shipping details when checking out to prevent losses in mail or other mishaps from happening.

Buyer no longer wants the items - we are not able to accept this reason of refund. The buyer should make sure he or she wants to buy the items before submitting an order, not after. After an order is submitted, the buyer enters into a legally binding contract with the seller to purchase all items in that order.

Buyer found items cheaper somewhere else - Buyer should be confident that he or she is ready to pay the asking prices before submitting an order. After an order is submitted, the buyer enters into a legally binding contract with the seller to purchase all items in that order.

Late or missing refunds (if applicable):

If you haven’t receive your approved refund within 15 working days from our refund approval email, kindly contact your credit card company to verify on the it as different bank may take different amount of time to process our refund before it is officially posted in your statement. If you are paying with PayPal or via Bank Transfer, you may also check with the respective parties on the incoming refund. 

If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund, please kindly contact us at customercare@symplified.biz and we will try to assist you as much as we can.
